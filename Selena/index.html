La Mente de la Reina

Una experiencia inmersiva en 3D en la mente de Selena.

Tesis:

A través de un modelo digital interactivo, este proyecto explora las complejidades de la identidad de Selena Quintanilla al presentar distintas facetas de su vida personal y profesional —como artista, mujer mexicoamericana, y figura pública— que fueron moldeadas, incomprendidas o distorsionadas por los medios. Mediante un entorno virtual dividido en "habitaciones" simbólicas, se busca representar lo que quizás existía en su mundo interior, más allá de la narrativa impuesta por la fama.

Una Ventaja a Selena

This project is inspired by the layered portrayals of identity in texts such as A House on Mango Street, A Room of One’s Own, and I Am Not Your Perfect Mexican Daughter. Each work explores how space, silence, and self-perception shape a woman’s identity. Selena Quintanilla, though celebrated, remains partly enigmatic—much of her personal interiority is unknown to us. Through this immersive 3D experience, we imagine what it might have felt like to exist within her creative, cultural, and private spaces. Like Julia Reyes in Erika Sánchez’s novel, Selena’s journey is one of defining herself on her own terms, away from external expectations. This project reflects on that quiet revelation of self.
