About

I am an undergraduate student at Kansas State University, pursuing a Bachelors of Science in Computer Science and Bachelors of Arts in Spanish. I'm currently working as an undergraduate teaching assistant and Department Ambassador at KSU on top of facilitating professional development as the president of the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM), and Diversity in Computing (DiC[ACM-W]). I'm also having a blast helping organize and photographing for Hack K-State! As an ongoing side quest, I've acquired 7+ years of experience working as a theatrical Lighting Designer, dabbling with a bit of sound engineering for local musicals. My goal is is to eventually innovate at the intersection of Theater, Music, Media, Language & Technology in a manner that enhances and empowers the voices of others.
